bio

I am a producer/beatmaker born in Connecticut and recently graduated from Berklee College of Music with a degree in Music Production. My musical trajectory was heavily influenced by Jazz music when I discovered it in Middle School and it is what initially brought me to Berklee.

Since studying music in school I have developed a new appreciation and ear for other styles of music such Electronic genres and Hip-Hop, to name a couple. In everything I make and do I try to not too tightly confine myself to modern tropes and always do what I can to make myself feel emotional in the process.
